We want an agent workflow to help our support team create better engineering handoffs.

Right now support tickets are inconsistent. Some are rich with evidence and some are vague. Engineering wastes time trying to figure out what happened before they can start debugging.

The workflow should:
- summarize the customer issue
- pull out reproduction clues
- highlight missing information
- draft a structured handoff for engineering

Constraints:
- it should escalate weak tickets instead of pretending they are ready
- a human should review anything labeled high priority
- we need a pilot-friendly first version
