We are exploring an AI workflow for executive support.

Executives forward messy notes, Slack messages, and email threads and want a clean briefing with action items, decision points, and missing context.

The workflow should:
- normalize incoming material
- produce a concise briefing
- separate confirmed facts from inferred points
- recommend what needs human review before anything is sent onward

Constraints:
- no autonomous sending
- fast turnaround matters
- outputs need to stay short and practical
